About

Entrepreneur & Software Engineer

Victory Friday is an Entrepreneur and software developer. She has founded five start-ups although Safe Travels is her first and most successful. She founded Safe Travels during the pandemic in May 2020. Safe Travels was created to help customers book tickets to and from their various destinations during Covid.
With Safe Travels now in over 50 countries in the world and steadily growing, the company has been able to reach a sustainable goal and serve not only as a booking agent, but transform and adjust with the time.
Safe Travels books flights, process student, work and tourist visas, and has just recently launched her own transport system. We take customers to their beautiful destinations, and create magic experiences that last for a lifetime.
